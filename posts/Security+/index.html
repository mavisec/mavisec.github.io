🔐 What is CompTIA Security+?

CompTIA Security+ is a globally recognized entry-level certification that validates your understanding of essential cybersecurity concepts. It’s often the first cert people earn when starting their careers in IT or cybersecurity. Many employers (including government jobs) accept Security+ as a baseline requirement. Exam Code: SY0-701 (launched in Nov 2023) Duration: 90 minutes Questions: Up to 90 Question Types: Multiple choice + performance-based Passing Score: 750/900 Price: ~$392 USD (discount vouchers available online)
